Oatmeal

Bowl of oatmeal

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup oats
  • 1 cup water
  • Pinch salt
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the oats. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the oats are soft and have absorbed most of the liquid, about 5 minutes. Add the milk, remove from heat, cover and let stand for 2-3 minutes.
